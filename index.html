About this tool

web-video-trans is a free browser-based video and audio transcription tool for musicians, learners, and close listeners. It helps you open local media files, loop difficult phrases, slow playback, inspect waveforms, apply EQ presets, and view a piano-roll style transcription without uploading your files to a server.

Key features

  • Open local video and audio files directly in the browser
  • Set A/B loops for repeated listening
  • Slow down playback for hard passages
  • Zoom into the waveform for precise seeking
  • Use EQ presets to focus on instruments like bass, guitar, piano, vocal, and drums
  • Transcribe short passages into a piano-roll note view
  • Keep files local for privacy and speed
