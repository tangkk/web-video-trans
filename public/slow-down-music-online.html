Slow Down Music Online

A free browser tool for slowing music down so you can hear difficult phrases more clearly. Open local video or audio files, reduce playback speed, set A/B loops, and inspect details without switching into a full production workflow.

Open the tool View source on GitHub

When slowing music down helps

  • Fast melodic runs that blur together at full speed
  • Dense bass or piano movement inside a full mix
  • Hard rhythmic placement and articulation details
  • Transcription practice when you need repeated close listening

Playback speed control is most useful when it is combined with tight looping and clear visual navigation. That is why web-video-trans pairs slower playback with waveform zoom, precise seeking, and short phrase transcription support.

Why use this instead of a heavy editor

Sometimes you do not need a full DAW session. You just need to open a file quickly, find the phrase, slow it down, and listen until it makes sense. This tool is designed for exactly that kind of lightweight practice and transcription workflow.
