What an A/B loop player is for

An A/B loop player lets you define a start point and an end point, then replay the same phrase continuously. This is one of the most useful listening tools when learning music by ear, checking articulation, copying bass movement, or understanding timing inside a difficult run.

Why use web-video-trans for A/B looping

Besides A/B looping, the main tool also gives you waveform inspection, playback speed control, EQ presets, and a short phrase transcription view. That makes it more useful than a plain repeat player when you are actually trying to figure out notes, rhythm, or articulation.
